## Introduction
A few years ago, I was the person with a nightstand full of unread books and a ready excuse about being too busy. A friend changed that by handing me a worn thriller and saying, “Just ten pages before sleep. No pressure.” That one tiny habit turned me into a 47-book-a-year reader—and I haven’t looked back.

2025 is already a rich year for reading, with fresh releases and rediscovered backlist titles sparking conversations everywhere. In this post, I’ve curated the books that are genuinely worth your precious downtime, covering fiction, non-fiction, and self-development. I’ll also share the practical, no-fuss habits that helped me read consistently without stealing hours from my day. Let’s dive in.
